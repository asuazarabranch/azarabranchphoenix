Our Mission

Community is Synonymous with Service

Mission Statement: Azara Branch is a registered 501(c)3 Non-Profit Organization that provides utilities for heat/homeless relief through providing utilities and supplies to the unsheltered Phoenix population of District 7 and beyond.

We want to encourage students to provide aid for local homeless communities on-site. Volunteering on-site allows students to make a positive impact not just through providing physical goods, but also having direct & meaningful conversations with the people they are helping.
